## Template Usage

**When to create CONTINUATION.md:**
- Starting work that will take >30 minutes
- Complex multi-file changes
- Work that might be interrupted (compaction, Mom needs you, etc.)
- Experiments or explorations you want to pause/resume

**When to clear CONTINUATION.md:**
- Task completed (mark status as "clear")
- Work abandoned (document why in TASKQUEUE.md)
- Handed off to different session (note in NOW.md)

**Keep it updated:**
- Update progress after each major step
- Note new files as you create them
- Add decisions and reasoning as you go
- Brief > exhaustive (future-you just needs continuity)
